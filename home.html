Our History

Atari was founded by Nolan Bushnell and Ted Dabney in 1972 and was a pioneer of arcade games, home video game consoles, and personal computers. The company produced groundbreaking hits like Pong and Asteroids.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring joy and nostalgia to gamers worldwide by reviving classic titles and creating innovative new experiences that capture the spirit of our legendary past.
